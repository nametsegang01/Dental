Oral Health Tips for Students

1. Brush Twice Daily

Make it a habit to brush your teeth in the morning and before bedtime to keep your mouth fresh and clean.

2. Don’t Skip Flossing

Flossing removes plaque and food particles between your teeth that a toothbrush can't reach.

3. Cut Down on Sugary Snacks

Limit soda, candy, and processed snacks that can lead to cavities and enamel damage.

4. Drink More Water

Water helps rinse away bacteria and keeps your mouth hydrated — especially after meals.

5. Don’t Miss Your Dental Appointments

Visit the student dental clinic regularly for cleanings and check-ups — even if your teeth feel fine.

6. Replace Your Toothbrush

Change your toothbrush every 3 months or when bristles become frayed.

7. Use Fluoride Toothpaste

Fluoride helps strengthen your enamel and protects against cavities.
